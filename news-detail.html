I Congress of Endourologists of Central Asia Held in Tashkent

On June 21, 2025, the Ministry of Health of Uzbekistan hosted the First Congress of Endourologists of Central Asia in Tashkent. The event brought together leading specialists from across the region and internationally to discuss current advancements in urology, share experience, and strengthen regional cooperation in healthcare.

This congress marked a milestone in strengthening professional collaboration in Central Asia's medical community.

The congress featured presentations on minimally invasive surgery, innovations in urological care, and development strategies in endourology. The event was widely covered by official media and is available for viewing online via the Ministry’s public communications portal.
