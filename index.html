Welcome to Uzbekistan Infectious Disease Association

The Uzbekistan Infectious Disease Association (UIDA) is a non-governmental professional organization that unites infectious disease specialists, phthisiologists, and epidemiologists across Uzbekistan.

Our mission is to promote scientific excellence, advance medical education, and support public health initiatives focused on infectious and communicable diseases.

Minimum Requirements for Membership

  • 1. At least 3 publications in peer-reviewed journals (e.g., indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, or approved by the Higher Attestation Commission (HAC))
  • 2. Master’s degree or residency in Infectious Diseases, Epidemiology, or Phthisiology "Pulmonologist"
  • 3. Participation in at least one scientific or clinical conference (regional or international)
  • 4. Completed application form with valid contact information

Note: Applications are reviewed by our expert committee. Qualified professionals will be issued an official certificate of membership.
